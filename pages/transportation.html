Transportation

There are a variety of transportation options to not only get to the island, but once you are here as well. To get to the island, you currently have three options: a small jet plane from from another local island, a small propeller plane from a nearby island, or a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Bay for one night per week. Soon, we plan to expand the airport to accommodate larger jets to land on the island. Once you are here, you have many options for exploring the island. Many tourist stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing, which is relatively easy to explore on foot. However, if you wanted to venture futher or just have other options; there is plenty to choose from. Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m to 11 p.m. daily. Private busses serve the rest of the island. Taxis are available in the city, and cars can be rented from a local agency near the aitport. Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law).

To the Island

BlueWave Air

A premium regional airline offering quick, comfortable jet service to Taniti from nearby major cities. Known for its panoramic window views and complimentary tropical drinks on board. BlueWave is perfect for business travelers and vacationers alike.

Island Hopper Air

A casual, scenic airline using prop planes to fly between neighboring islands and Taniti. Flights are low and slow - perfect for taking in the truquoise waters and rugged coastlines. Great for adventurous travelers who love the charm of flying close to the ocean.

Azure Horizons Cruises

Azure Horizons offers a luxurious week-long tropical voyage aboard the MV Celestia, a mid-sized cruise ship designed for relaxation and exploration. The Celestia docks every Thursday at Taniti's main port, making this perfect for anyone wanting to enjoy a week or two exploring lush jungles, snorkling in coral reefs, and enjoying cultural performances.

On the Island

Taniti Breeze Transit

Running daily from 5 AM to 11PM, Taniti's Breeze Transit is the island's reliable and affordable public bus service. With scenic coastal routes and convenient stops in all major towns, beaches and tourist areas, it's the easiest way for locals and visitors to explore Taniti without a car.

Island Comfort Shuttles

Offering air-conditioned buses with plush seating, Island Comfort Shuttles provides private transportation for resorts, tour groups, and event planners. Whether it's a guided excursion to hidden waterfalls or a direct ride to the airport, this company ensures a smooth and stress free journey.

Taniti Quick Cab

Need a fat, friendly ride? Taniti Quick Cab operates a fleet of bright yellow taxis ready to take you anywhere on the island. Available for airport pickups, hotel transfers, and late-night rides, all cabs are equipped with GPS and set-rate pricing to make every trip easy and hassle-free.

Island Wheels Car Rentals

Explore Taniti at your own pave with Island Wheels Car Rentals! Offering a wide range of vehicles - from compact cars for easy city driving to compact cars for easy city driving to rugged SUVs perfect for exploring the island's off-the-beaten-path destinations. With a convenient pickup location by the airport, afforable rates, and 24/7 roadside assistance, Island Wheels ensures a smooth and hassle-free experience for every traveler.

Coastal Pedals

Exploring Taniti on two wheels? Coastal Pedals rents high-quality bikes and helmets (required by law) for a safe and fun ride. Choose from city bikes, mountain bikes, or electric models, and cruise the island's scenic trails and beachfront roads with ease. Daily and weekly rentals available.

*You can also find bike rentals from locals along the island.
