Dining & Drinking

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine. Every restaurant on the island has its own unique spin on "tasteful and amazing". There are also several pubs on the island, including a microbrewery.
